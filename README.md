. Change the small switch on the bottom left of the module to 12C
2.(Optional) If using a external speaker plug it in to the SPK2 port on the right of the module and change the bottom right switch to SPK2
3. Plug the 4 wire cord into the module and on the other side plug a male-to-male wire and insert it into the bread board EXACTLY like this:
       Green (Top)--> A4
       Blue (Second to top) --> A5
       Black (Second to Bottom) --> GND
       Red (Bottom) --> VCC (5v)
4. Turn on the Nano. you should hear a voice prompt like "Your welcome to use the voice recognition assistant"
5. To turn on the robot, say "Hello Robot" and it should respond
6. If you would like to test it, say "Turn Volume Down" and it should do just that.
7. Say "Learning command word" and repeat what you to say to fire a dart when prompted (should be 3 times). Once it says "learning complete" or something like that remain silent until it turns off.
